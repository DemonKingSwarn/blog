Hey everyone, 

so today i want to tell you all how i started touch typing. so over the years, i never bothered to take touch typing seriously. even
back then in school when out computer lab was teaching touch typing, i subsonciously developed my own method of typing and over the years
i just kept on improving it and i actually get to 80wpm and the funny part was i looked at the keyboard here and there.

so i started touch typing during last month Feb of 2026, and i was frustrated as my speed dropped but over the time its only march and
i can go till 78wpm already. and while i was practicing touch typing i found out about home row mods, so i did started using that.
because i use a normal keyboard which is [Evofox Katana X2](https://www.amazon.in/EvoFox-Mechanical-Replaceable-Backlighting-Anti‑Ghosting/dp/B0FDGK9FV3/520-5367969-5889753?pd_rd_w=oAacK&content-id=amzn1.sym.2fa5ef78-d215-4b54-bdb7-fa3d3620b822&pf_rd_p=2fa5ef78-d215-4b54-bdb7-fa3d3620b822&pf_rd_r=D0BWX6XSRVFA7BHAM5S7&pd_rd_wg=qqfH7&pd_rd_r=12002b36-aec1-40a6-b310-ddaff319d85a&pd_rd_i=B0FDGK9FV3&th=1) i had to use [kanata](https://github.com/jtroo/kanata)
to achieve the same level of customization a QMK keyboard provides.

so i started learning touch typing using the sites [edclub](https://edclub.com), [keybr](https://keybr.com), and [monkeytype](https://monkeytype.com).

honestly, i never thought in just two months i would reach 78wpm even after changing the entire style, lets see where it will take me.

anyways, that's all i have to say today. i will see you guys on the next blog, take care and good bye.
